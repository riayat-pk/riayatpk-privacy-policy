# riayatpk-privacy-policy

RiayatPk ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RiayatPk mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

RiayatPk does not require users to create an account and we do not directly collect personal information such as names, phone numbers, or email addresses.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically.

Automatically Collected Information

Third-party services used in the app may collect information such as:

Device type and device identifiers

Advertising ID

IP address

App interaction data

Approximate location derived from IP address

This information is used primarily for advertising, analytics, and improving user experience.

2. Advertising

RiayatPk displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google.

AdMob may collect and use data such as advertising identifiers, device information, and app interaction data to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google’s privacy policy.

RiayatPk aggregates and displays publicly available product information from various brand websites to help users discover deals and product details.

The information displayed in the app is publicly accessible.

RiayatPk is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected with any of the brands displayed.

All trademarks and brand names belong to their respective owners.

4. Data Storage

Product information retrieved from public sources may be stored locally on the user's device to improve app performance and reduce loading times.

RiayatPk does not store personal user information on its servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that collect information used to identify users or provide services. These may include:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

6. Children's Privacy

RiayatPk is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
